# The Marked — Rewritten GDD Export

This export rewrites the GDD around the updated direction:

- direct player movement from the start
- cross-platform horror idle ARPG, not strictly mobile
- grim illustrated dark fantasy art direction
- no chibi or cute mobile-idle proportions
- existing asset concepts may be preserved, but style must be revised if too cartoon/chibi
- The Marked is the player identity
- the Monolith observes living behavior and mutates the world
