# The Marked - Current GDD Alignment

This pack uses the rewritten GDD exported on 2026-06-27 as the source of truth.

## Locked Direction

- Game: The Marked
- Genre target: grim 2D horror idle ARPG with direct movement from the start
- MVP region: The Keeping House
- Camera target: 2.5D side-angled illustrated ARPG unless a future full asset pipeline supports isometric
- Player: The Marked, a haunted wanderer noticed by the Monolith
- Visual tone: grim illustrated dark fantasy, heavy ink, dramatic shadows, rough brush texture, desaturated palette, wax-red accents

## Non-Negotiables

- The player must physically move in the world from the beginning.
- Combat happens in physical spaces, not only a static menu battle screen.
- Existing generated concepts can be preserved, but cute/chibi/mobile-cartoon styling must be revised.
- Horror systems must stay readable and gameplay-facing.
- Observation/Witnessing is the unique hook and should affect assets, UI, enemies, corpses, signals, and rituals.

## Asset Preservation

Keep these concepts from the generated draft library:

- The Marked identity
- The Keeping House archive region
- Cabinet Indexer
- Ink Redactor
- Paper Wraith
- Witness Chair
- Seal Mother
- ink, paper, wax, debt, records, corpses, signals, rituals, and observation motifs

## Required Style Correction

Revise assets away from:

- chibi proportions
- oversized cute heads
- toy-like bodies
- bubbly mobile-idle silhouettes
- bright cheerful reward styling
- soft mascot expressions
- bouncy comedic motion

Revise assets toward:

- 5.5 to 7 heads tall for humanoids where possible
- grounded posture and visible weight
- severe silhouettes
- heavy shadow blocks
- distressed inked outlines
- parchment, bone, black ink, iron, wax seal, candlelight, rotted cloth, glass-eye, and red-thread materials

## MVP Asset Coverage

The rebuilt pack should support:

- controllable Marked character
- walk, idle, attack, hit, death/collapse, interact/pickup, ritual, low-sanity/channel animations
- left/right sprite flipping
- depth-based up/down movement on a walkable floor plane
- enemy aggro, hit reactions, attacks, deaths, and boss states
- loot drops, gear icons, ritual objects, signal objects, corpse states, zone transitions
- sanity, observation, tarot, reality trait, profile, inventory, ritual, signal, corpse, and Monolith UI assets

## Current Draft Verdict

The previous `the_marked_AA_core_asset_pack` and `keeping_house_AA_cartoon_animated_pack` are now concept drafts. They contain useful enemy and system ideas, but their player proportions and cartoon/mobile feel do not fully satisfy the current GDD.
